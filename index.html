Three years ago I thought consulting and marketing were different jobs. Turns out the gap between them is mostly just output format — one delivers decks, the other delivers campaigns, both have to be right about something first.

Right now I’m doing an MSc in Digital Marketing, interning at a consulting firm, and teaching myself machine learning in whatever time is left. The overlap between the three is where I actually want to live.

I get curious about how brands make decisions — not just what to post or how to grow, but whether the thing they’re building actually connects to what anyone wants. AI keeps making that question harder to avoid, which is exactly why I like it.
